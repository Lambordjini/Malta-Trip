☀️ Malta in July — what to expect
Hot, dry, and sunny every single day. Average 33–36°C, sea around 26°C. Virtually zero rain. Strong sun from 10AM–4PM.

Sun & UV guide
🕗
Best outdoor hours
Plan walks, hikes, and sightseeing for before 11:00 or after 17:00. Between noon and 4PM the heat is brutal — beaches and shade only.
🧴
SPF 50 is not optional
UV index in Malta in July regularly hits 10–11 (extreme). Reapply every 90 min. Even cloudy days are burning days.
💧
Hydration
Drink at least 2–3L of water a day. Malta tap water is safe but tastes brackish — buy large bottles at a supermarket (~€0.40 for 2L). Don't rely on cafes.
🌬️
The Xlokk wind
Occasionally a hot southerly wind (Xlokk) blows from Africa, pushing temps above 38°C. If it hits, go full beach/swimming day — don't fight it.
